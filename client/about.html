Gameplay:


Your letter score will be on your letter tile.
You will have 7 letter tiles on your tile rack in order to play.
You will be able to change the board designs to play on different boards.
After each round the tile rack will be filled up to maximum of 7 letter tiles.

Your score will be shown on the top right hand corner of the web page.
There is a information box which will present you with any relevant warnings or messages.
You can also be awarded special tile points which can be seen on the scrabble board. This will double or tripple the letters you play or the words you form.

Enjoy the game!
